Capital case letters are used to indicate stressed vowels in words. Where possible blue color is used to indicate masculine gender, pink color is used for feminine gender, and green color is used for neuter gender.

Профессия - Occupation

Most occupations in Russian language are only used in masculine gender even if they are used for female. However, there are a few occupations that are only used in feminine gender as they are mostly related to female occupation (балерина-ballerina, медсестра-nurse, няня-nanny)

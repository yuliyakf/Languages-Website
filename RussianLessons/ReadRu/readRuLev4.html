There are 33 letters in Russian alphabet.
Yellow color will be used for vowel letters. Blue color will be used for consonant letters.

Russian Cases Answers the Questions 1st Subject 2nd Subject 3rd Subject
1. Nominative - primary case, the subject. He is my friend. Who? What? (Кто? Что?) Я, Ты
I, You		 Мы, Вы
We, You		 Он/Оно, Она, Они
He/It, She, They
2. Genitive - indicates possession or origin. Book of his. Who is not here? (Нет кого?)
What is not here? (Нет чего?)		 Меня, Тебя
Me, You		 Нас, Вас
Us, You		 Его, Её, Их
His, Hers, Yours
3. Dative - recipient of giving, sending or saying something. Send a message to him. Give to whom? (Дать кому?)
Give to what? (Дать чему?)		 Мне, Тебе
To me, To you		 Нам, Вам
To us, To you		 Ему, Ей, Им
To him, To her, To them
4. Accusative - indicates direct object of an action or feeling. Mary doesn't like him. Blame whom? (Винить кого?)
Blame what?(Винить что?)		 Меня, Тебя
Me, You		 Нас, Вас
Us, You		 Его, Её, Их
Him, Her, Them
5. Instrumental - by means of. The book is written by him. Made by whom? (Кем?)
Made with what? (Чем?)		 Мной, Тобой
By me, By you		 Нами, Вами
By us, By you		 Им, Ею, Ими
By him, By her, By them
6. Prepositional - always used with preposition. Bob mentioned about her. Thinking of whom? (О ком?)
Thinking of what? (О чём?)		 Обо мне, О тебе
About me, About you		 О нас, О вас
About us, About you		 О Нём, О ней, О них
About him, About her, About them

All Russian nouns have gender. They are either masculine, feminine, or neuter. Once you learn to recognize different genders of nouns you will be able to understand how adjectives and verbs change depending on a noun's gender.

For learning purpose we will use blue color to identify masculine nouns in singular form, pink color for feminine nouns in singular form, green color for neuter nouns in singular form.

Masculine

- All nouns in a singular form that end in a consonant letter.
Стол (table), Друг (friend), Дом (house)

- All nouns in a singular form that end in letter "й"
Чай (tea), Музей (museum)

- Most nouns in a singular form that end in soft sign "ь"
День (day), Словарь (dictionary), Гость(guest)

- Few nouns (usually directly related to a male) in a singular form that end in letters "а" "я"
Папа (father), Мужчина (man), Дедушка(grandfather), Дядя (uncle)

Feminine

- Most nouns in a singular form that end in letters "а" "я"
Книга (book), Рука (arm), Девочка (girl)

- Some nouns in a singular form that end in soft sign "ь"
Лошадь (horse), Ночь (night)

Neuter

- Most nouns in a singular form that end in letters "о" "е"
Дерево (tree), Солнце (sun), Море (sea)

- There are some exceptions where words ending in letters "о" "е" are masculine
Кофе (coffee)


The ending of an adjective will change depending on the gender of a noun.

Masculine

End in "ой" "ый" "ий"
Большой дом (big house), Сильный мужчина (strong man), Маленький мальчик (small boy)

Feminine

End in "ая" "яя"
Красивая женщина (beautiful woman), Старшая сестра (older sister), Молодая жена (young wife)

Neuter

End in "ое" "ее"
Высокое дерево (tall tree), Синее небо (blue sky), Большое поле (big field)


Now let's see how to change singular nouns to plural form

Masculine

In most cases add "и" or "ы"
Стол - Столы(tables), Мальчик - Мальчики(boys), Дождь - Дожди(rain)

There are a few exceptions when the word will change the ending to "а" "ья" or will transform the root of a word
Дом - Дома(houses), Стул - Стулья(chairs), День - Дни(days)

Feminine

The ending "а" "я" "ь" will change to "ы" or "и"
Птица - Птицы(birds), Тетрадь - Тетради(notebooks), Школа - Школы(schools)

Neuter

The ending "о" will change to "а". The ending "е" will change to "я".
Море - Моря(seas), Дело - Дела(businesses), Поле - Поля(fields)

There are a few exceptions
Яблоко - Яблоки(apples), Крыло - Крылья(wings)

There are some exceptions with words that only have singular form or only plural form

Only Singular

цемЕнт(cement), сАхар(sugar), чАй(tea), серебрО(silver), мЁд(honey), дЕтство(childhood), молодЁежь(youth), молокО(milk), мАсло(butter), темнотА(darkness), водА(water), дрУжба(friendship)

Only Plural-do not have gender

слИвки(cream), дрОжжи(yeast), ворОта(gate), брЮки(pants), качЕли(swing), нОжницы(scissors), часЫ(watch), лЮди(people), очкИ(eye-glasses)

The ending of adjectives adjoining to plural nouns will change to "ые" "ие"
